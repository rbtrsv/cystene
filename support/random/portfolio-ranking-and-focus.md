# Portfolio — Ranking & Focus Allocation

Analiza onesta a 4 proiecte din portofoliu. Scop: prioritizare timp si efort.

---

## Ranking combinat (face bani + are viitor + interes)

### 1. Finpy — cel mai probabil sa faca bani

**Piata**: investment tools = wallet-uri adanci. VC-uri, family offices, syndicate leads platesc fara sa se uite.

**Avantaje reale:**
- Pricing $6/entitate agresiv disruptiv vs Carta/Pulley/AngelList ($500-5000/mo)
- MCP-first = nisa noua, nu e deja ocupata (Carta nu are MCP)
- Micro-funds, syndicate leads, angel networks sub $50M AUM sunt underserved — Carta e overkill, Excel e subpar
- LTV mare ($100-1000/mo, retentie 5+ ani)

**Risc principal**: trust & compliance. Cap table software = data juridica (409A, securities law). VC-uri cer SOC2, audit trail bullet-proof. Solo-founder trust gap e real.

**Estimare realista**: $500K-3M ARR in nisa sub-Carta. Top tier blocat de compliance.

---

### 2. Nexotype — cel mai bun viitor, drum mai greu la bani

**Avantaje reale:**
- AI + knowledge graph = directia corecta tehnologic pentru urmatorii 10 ani
- 61 entitati + grounded answers + MCP = arhitectural mai puternic decat Cystene/Nudgio
- Dual positioning B2B/B2C (biotech intel + longevity)
- Pricing $20/$120/$360 — $360 e efectiv gratuit pentru pharma

**Risc principal**: data moat (nu ai echipa de PhD sa curezi) + clinical credibility (pharma nu cumpara fara MD/PhD pe echipa). B2C longevity e sange pe podea (InsideTracker/Function/Nebula).

**Estimare realista**: $100K-1M ARR. Potential real de unicorn daca prinzi co-founder clinical + data partnerships.

---

### 3. Cystene — proiectul interesant intelectual

**Realitate dura:**
- Concurenti: Tenable ($11B cap), Rapid7 ($3B), Qualys ($5B), Vanta ($2.5B pentru compliance)
- Cei 12 scanner types — fiecare e un produs de sine statator. Tenable are 1000+ ingineri pe asta
- CISOs nu schimba vendor fara certificari (SOC2 al tool-ului de security, nu doar al clientului)
- Compliance (SOC2/ISO27001) deja dominat de Vanta/Drata/Secureframe cu $100M+ funding fiecare

**De ce se tine peste Nudgio in alocare activa:**
- Security evolueaza constant (CVEs noi, vectori noi) = profunzime intelectuala infinita
- AI-native security scanning = trend real (agents autonomi, LLM pentesting)
- Interesul e multiplicator pe proiect solo pe 2+ ani — plictiseala ucide mai repede decat market fit slab
- Poti atrage community tehnica (open-source security researchers)

**Singur drum viabil**: wedge foarte ingust. Un singur scanner type, un singur persona, un singur vertical. Ex: "AI-powered SSL/TLS monitoring pentru SaaS startups sub 50 angajati, $29/mo".

**Estimare realista**: $50-300K ARR ca dev tool. Enterprise locked out fara certificari.

---

### 4. Nudgio — launched passive, zero efort activ

**Status curent (aprilie 2026):**
- App public submis in Shopify App Store (status: Submitted, in review)
- 1-2 installs din dev stores
- 22 versiuni release in ultima luna (dev terminat, overengineering potential)
- $0 earnings

**Probleme de baza (nu au disparut):**
- Shopify Magic (native AI recs, gratis) in theme-uri noi
- Klaviyo + Shopify absorb categoria
- Algolia, Nosto, Bloomreach, Dynamic Yield, LimeSpot, Rebuy = zeci de concurenti maturi
- "Recommendation engine" = commoditate in 2026

**Efort ramas = aproape zero:**
- Astepti raspuns Shopify review (2-8 saptamani)
- Raspunzi la feedback reviewer (1-3 zile per iteratie)
- Dupa approval: listing live, installs organic posibile
- NU adaugi features noi. NU incepi marketing activ. Lasi pasiv.

**Estimare realista**: $20-100K ARR in cel mai bun caz (bonus pasiv). Daca in 6 luni post-approval e <50 installs activi, arhiva.

---

## Alocare timp finala

- **50% Finpy** — shipping de revenue cel mai rapid; fereastra MCP-native deschisa acum
- **30% Nexotype** — pariu pe viitor; continua pana prinzi co-founder clinical / data partnership
- **15% Cystene** — proiectul interesant; doar cu wedge ingust definit
- **5% Nudgio** — doar pentru Shopify review response; 0% dupa approval

### Traducere in ore (la 50 ore/saptamana)

- Finpy: ~25h/saptamana
- Nexotype: ~15h/saptamana
- Cystene: ~7h/saptamana
- Nudgio: ~2h/saptamana (scade la 0h dupa approval)

---

## Rationale

**De ce 50/30 nu 30/50:**
Finpy are combinatia rara: (a) nevoie reala + (b) wallet adanc + (c) diferentiator MCP + (d) pricing disruptiv. Nexotype are thesis-ul mai bun pe 10 ani, dar bani imediati vin mai greu (data moat, clinical trust gap).

**De ce Cystene peste Nudgio (desi Nudgio are ARR potential similar):**
Interesul e multiplicator pe outcome in proiect solo. Plictiseala ucide. Cystene are substanta intelectuala, Nudgio e pattern matching pe order history. Pe 12-18 luni de grind fara traction, motivatia conteaza mai mult decat market fit teoretic.

**De ce Nudgio 5% si nu 0%:**
E deja submis in Shopify review. Costul de a-l duce la live = ~5-10h total in urmatoarele 2 luni. Dupa approval, orice install = bonus pasiv. Nu are sens sa-l ucizi acum cand e aproape la linia de finish.

## Plan concret pe Cystene (inainte sa construiesti mai mult)

Raspunde onest:
1. Un **singur** scanner type pe care te concentrezi? (SSL? web? port? DNS?)
2. **Un** persona target? (freelance dev agency? small SaaS? devops in-house?)
3. **O** problema concreta rezolvata mai bine decat Tenable/Detectify?
4. Cum ajung primii 10 clienti fara ads? (cold outreach? community? open source? content?)

Daca nu ai raspunsuri clare la toate 4, **NU mai scrie cod**. 2 saptamani doar customer discovery (LinkedIn DMs, forumuri security, 10 interviuri target). Apoi construiesti.

**Leapadare necesara**: 12 scanner types = 12 products = solo impossible. Alege 1-2 maxim. Sterge restul din landing.

---

## Plan concret pe Nudgio (post-launch pasiv)

1. **STOP dev activ**. 22 versiuni in 1 luna = overengineering.
2. **Monitor Shopify Partners email** pentru review response. Raspunzi prompt (24-48h).
3. **Pregateste listing assets** (screenshots, icon, demo 60s) daca lipsesc.
4. **NU adauga features noi** pana nu iei primul client real platit.
5. **NU marketing activ** pana nu esti Approved + Listed.
6. **3 luni dupa approval**: check installs + conversie.
   - HIT (>100 installs + 10 paid): escaladeaza la 15%
   - MISS: lasa pe autopilot, arhiva dupa 6 luni daca nimic.

---

## Adevarul dur

Finpy + Nexotype sunt cele doua pariuri reale. Cystene e proiectul personal interesant — justificat daca iti pastreaza motivatia pe 12+ luni. Nudgio e aproape live si pasiv, nu merita omorat acum. Concentrarea reala trebuie pe primele doua.
